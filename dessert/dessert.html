DESSERT: NUTELLA MUG CAKE

This warm nutella mug cake is rich, chocolatey, and ready to eat in just a few minutes. This late-night treat is great for when you're craving something sweet but don't have time to spend hours in the kitchen.

Image Credit: "Protein Mug Cake" by Maya Krampf at https://www.wholesomeyum.com/protein-mug-cake/.

GROCERY LIST

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp nutella
  • strawberries

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder in a microwave-safe mug.
  2. Add the milk and vegetable oil, then stir until smooth.
  3. Mix in the nutella until fully combined.
  4. Microwave for about 60–90 seconds, or until the cake is cooked through.
  5. Top with some fresh cut strawberries.
  6. Let it cool for 1–2 minutes before eating.

FUN FACT

The name "Nutella" originated from combining "nut" which represents the spread's signature ingredient (hazelnuts), with the Italian feminine diminutive suffix "-ella" which means "small" or "sweet"!
