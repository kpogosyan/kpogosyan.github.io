LUNCH: TURKEY SANDWICH

This classic turkey sandwich is fresh, flavorful, and can be thrown together quickly with just a few simple ingredients. Packed with turkey, crispy bacon, avocado spread, and cheddar cheese, it's a lunch that will leave you feeling totally satisfied.

Image Credit: "Grilled California Turkey Bistro Sandwich" by Land O'Lakes at https://www.landolakes.com/recipe/7899/grilled-california-turkey-bistro-sandwich/.

GROCERY LIST

  • 2 slices of bread
  • butter
  • avocado spread
  • 4 slices of deli turkey
  • 2 slices of cooked bacon
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Lightly toast the bread in butter.
  2. Spread a generous layer of avocado spread onto one slice of bread.
  3. Layer the turkey, bacon, and cheddar cheese on top.
  4. Place the second slice of bread on top.
  5. Slice the sandwich in half.

FUN FACT

The sandwich is named after John Montagu, the 4th Earl of Sandwich. You can choose to believe this or not, but legend says he requested this meal so that he could eat with one hand while gambling or working without getting his cards greasy or taking a break!
