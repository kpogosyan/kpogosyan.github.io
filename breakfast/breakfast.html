BREAKFAST: FRENCH TOAST

Golden and crispy on the outside, soft and fluffy on the inside, this french toast is the perfect way to start your morning. Topped with fresh berries and a drizzle of maple syrup, it's a delicious breakfast that's easy enough for anyone to make.

Image Credit: "Air Fryer French Toast" by Typhur Eats at https://explore.typhur.com/air-fryer-french-toast.

GROCERY LIST

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 slices of bread
  • butter
  • strawberries
  • blueberries
  • maple syrup
  • powdered sugar

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Whisk the eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon.
  2. Dip each slice of bread into the mixture.
  3. Melt butter in a pan.
  4. Cook each side for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Top with fresh strawberries and blueberries.
  6. Drizzle with maple syrup and a light dusting of powdered sugar.

FUN FACT

Despite the name, french toast was actually originated in ancient Rome, not France! The practice of soaking stale bread in an egg mixture before frying it was widespread across Europe long before France ever even existed. In France today, the dish is called pain perdu, meaning "lost bread".
