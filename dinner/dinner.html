DINNER: FETTUCCINE ALFREDO PASTA

This creamy fettuccine alfredo is super rich and easy to make. Coated in alfredo sauce and topped with parsley, it's the perfect classic dinner for any night of the week.

Image Credit: "Fettuccine Alfredo" by Love and Lemons at https://www.loveandlemons.com/fettuccine-alfredo-recipe/comment-page-3/.

GROCERY LIST

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • parsley

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to the package directions.
  2. Melt the butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and cook for about 1 minute.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and let it simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Mix in the parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth.
  6. Add the cooked pasta and toss until evenly coated.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper, then garnish with parsley.

FUN FACT

Italy produces between 1,700,000 and 3,300,000 tons of pasta per year! This is more than any other country and according to consumption data, Italians eat roughly 60 pounds of pasta per person every year. By comparison, an average American eats an average of about 26 pounds of pasta every year.
